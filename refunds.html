Refunds

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase.If for any reason you're not satisfied, we offer a refund policy.

Refund Policy

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery.

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team.

We will provide a return merchandise authorization (RMA) number.

Refund Process

  • Return the item(s) in their original condition.
  • Include all original packaging and accessories.
  • Ship the item(s) back to us within 30 days of delivery.
  • We will process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Refund Methods

We offer refunds in the original payment method.

Please note that refunds may take up to 10 business days to appear on your statement.
